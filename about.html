About Us

Global Trends Bubble is a web application that provides a real-time visualization of trending topics from around the world. Our interactive treemap helps you discover what's popular across technology, entertainment, sports, politics, and business sectors.

Our mission is to provide a unique and engaging way to explore the world's trending topics. We believe that by visualizing data, we can gain a deeper understanding of the world around us.
